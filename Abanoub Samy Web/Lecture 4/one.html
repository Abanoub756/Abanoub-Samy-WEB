HSL Lightness

The third parameter of hsl() defines the lightness. 0% means black, and 100% means white:

hsl(0, 100%, 66%)

hsl(120, 100%, 25%)

hsl(245, 100%, 50%)

hsl(0, 100%, 75%)

hsl(0, 100%, 90%)

hsl(0, 100%, 100%)
